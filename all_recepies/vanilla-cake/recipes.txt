Vanilla cake

1 - mix the eggs and sugar
2 - infuse the milk with orange zest and cinnamon
3 - add the milk to the eggs and stir
4 - sift the flour
5 - mix carefully until fully incorporated
6 - let it rest for 15 minutes in the fridge
7 - bake for 45 minutes 180C
8 - sift some icing sugar on top before serving
