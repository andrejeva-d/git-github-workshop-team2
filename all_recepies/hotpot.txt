Spicy Sichuan-style broth

- 1.5–2 L stock
- 2–3 tbsp chili oil
- 1 tbsp Sichuan peppercorn
- 0.5 tbsp salt (cautious as sometimes doubanjiang also have salt)
- 2 dried chilies
- 1 tbsp doubanjiang (fermented chili bean paste)
- Garlic + ginger

Proteins

- Shrimp
- Scallops
- Mussels
- Clams
- Razor clams
- Tofu
- Beef
- Beef sliced

Veg

- Mushroom
- Lotus root
- Spinach

Carbs

- Rice
- Dumpings
